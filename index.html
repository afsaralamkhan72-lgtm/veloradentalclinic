Why Dentistry and Aesthetics Belong Together

Dr. Fatima Malak trained first as a dental surgeon, then pursued advanced certification in endodontics and implantology before completing a diploma in medical aesthetics. That order matters: it means every aesthetic decision at Velora is made with a clinician's understanding of facial structure, bite, and bone, not just skin.

She founded Velora to close a gap she kept seeing in Peshawar: patients travelling between a dental clinic and a separate aesthetic centre for care that is, in her view, deeply connected. Velora was built so a single consultation could address both, and so that every aesthetic procedure is overseen by someone with a full medical understanding of the face, not just its surface.

Patients describe her approach as unhurried and direct: an honest assessment first, a plan second, and never a treatment recommended simply because it's available.

Questions

Dental Care, Answered

Is root canal treatment painful?

Modern root canal therapy is performed under local anaesthesia and is designed to relieve pain, not cause it. Dr. Fatima is C-Endo certified specifically in this procedure.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care, implants can last decades. Your suitability and expected timeline are assessed during your first consultation.

Can I get braces as an adult?

Yes, orthodontic treatment at Velora is offered for children and adults alike, with options suited to different lifestyles.

Can I book a dental and aesthetic treatment in one visit?

Yes, mention both areas of interest during your consultation and Dr. Fatima will plan a visit that addresses each, where clinically appropriate.

Questions

Aesthetics, Answered

Are the aesthetic treatments performed by a certified physician?

Yes. Dr. Fatima Malak holds a diploma in medical aesthetics in addition to her dental qualifications, and personally oversees every aesthetic procedure at Velora.

How many sessions does HIFU or HydraFacial need?

This depends on your skin goals and is discussed at your consultation. Most patients see visible results within one to three sessions.

Is there downtime after treatment?

Most aesthetic treatments at Velora involve little to no downtime. Any recovery guidance specific to your treatment is explained beforehand.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

Your first visit is a consultation. Dr. Fatima will assess your concerns and recommend a plan, and there is no obligation to proceed the same day.
